# Manage Settings Guide

## Goal

Configure Alter defaults, appearance, dictation, permissions, and profile.

## Steps

1. Open Alter, then press `Cmd+,`.
2. Configure **Defaults** for quick access behavior.
3. Configure **Appearance** for notch visibility and theme.
4. Configure **Dictation** for processor, language, and replacements.
5. Configure **Permissions** and grant required macOS access.
6. Configure **Profile** for role, projects, and communication style.
