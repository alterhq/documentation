# Use Case: Team Onboarding with Alter

## Scenario

A team wants consistent prompts and quick setup for new members.

## Setup

- Shared guidance on model selection and settings
- A starter workspace with common context files

## Workflow

1. New member opens Alter and applies baseline settings.
2. Team shares workspace and naming conventions.
3. Member uses guided prompts for recurring tasks.
4. Team reviews outcomes and refines prompts weekly.

## Outcome

Faster onboarding, more consistent outputs, and fewer setup mistakes.
