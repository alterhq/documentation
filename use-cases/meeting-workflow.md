# Use Case: Meeting Capture and Follow-Up

## Scenario

An operator needs reliable transcript capture and quick action extraction.

## Setup

- Meeting recording enabled
- Dictation settings tuned for language and processor

## Workflow

1. Enable auto-record for meetings.
2. Capture transcript during call.
3. Use prompts to extract action items and owners.
4. Export or share summary with the team.

## Outcome

Higher meeting recall quality and faster post-meeting execution.
